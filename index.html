About Our Department

Established in 2005, the Department of Information and Communication Technology at Mangosuthu University of Technology is a leading institution in digital education. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice.

Our faculty consists of experienced professionals and researchers who bring real-world expertise into the classroom. We maintain strong partnerships with leading tech companies to ensure our curriculum remains relevant and cutting-edge.

Industry-aligned Curriculum

Our courses are continuously updated with input from industry partners including Microsoft, Cisco, and local tech companies.

Practical Laboratory Learning

24/7 access to state-of-the-art labs equipped with the latest hardware, software, and networking equipment.

Qualified Academic Staff

Learn from PhD-holding faculty with industry experience at companies like Google, IBM, and local enterprises.

Academic Programmes

Diploma in ICT

NQF Level 6 3 Years

Comprehensive foundation in programming, databases, web development, and systems analysis. Includes 6-month industry internship.

Extended Curriculum Programme

NQF Level 5-6 4 Years

Extended foundation year with additional academic support, followed by the standard diploma curriculum.

Advanced Diploma in ICT

NQF Level 7 1 Year

Specialized advanced studies focusing on emerging technologies, IT management, and research methodology.

Specialization Tracks

Software Development

Master modern programming languages including Python, Java, and JavaScript. Learn full-stack development, mobile app creation, cloud computing, and agile methodologies.

Career paths: Software Engineer, Full-Stack Developer, Mobile App Developer, DevOps Engineer

Networking & Infrastructure

Design, implement, and manage secure network infrastructures. Study routing, switching, network security, cloud networking, and wireless technologies.

Career paths: Network Engineer, Security Analyst, Cloud Architect, Systems Administrator
